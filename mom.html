HAPPY MOTHER'S DAY

Dear Mom,

I say a different variation of the same thing every year, when it comes to Mother's Day. The reason I end up saying the same things, like "I love you", "I appreciate you", or "You're the best mom I have"(haha), is because every year, those statements are still true. However, as I get older and we spend more time apart, I begin to realize how true and important these sayings are to me. Each day I don't get to see you makes me understand just how much I love you and how you are important to me.

This Year

This year has been a huge year in my life, switching to Computer Science has changed the course of my life and you have been so supportive of this change (I'm sure you're very happy that I switched). It's going to be a much more difficult road majoring in CS, but I'm not too worried. The reason why I'm not worried is because of the person you've raised me to be (plus I know you worry for me ... A LOT and we both don't need to worry for me). I'm not worried about all the work that lies ahead because you've taught me to be a hard working person, everything that I need to do will get done. I'm also not worried about all the new people I'll meet along the way because you've taught me to be caring and kind to others. Being kind is one of the best qualities you've instilled in me and it's the one I am most grateful for.

You are beautiful. You are caring. You are kind.

And I love you.

Love,

Bo
