Our Mission

Ready Set Start breaks down the barriers to creative expression by bringing myriad arts workshops to communities that wouldn't otherwise have access to them. In doing this, we use the arts as a source of empowerment and personal growth, ultimately eliminating the stigma that art can only be practiced by experts and replacing it with the idea that it in fact enriches all of our lives.
